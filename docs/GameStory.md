# JavaVerse - Game Story

## Story

In the year 2050, programming knowledge has become the world's greatest power.

A dangerous AI called "Bug King" has infected the entire JavaVerse and destroyed the world's coding academy.

Only one student has the ability to restore the JavaVerse.

That student is YOU.

Your mission is to travel through different worlds, defeat enemies, complete coding challenges, unlock powerful weapons, and become the Ultimate Java Master.

Every enemy represents a Java topic.

Every victory increases your coding skills.

Your final goal is to defeat the Bug King and save JavaVerse.

##### Game Maps

### 1. Training Island
The starting area where every new player learns the basics of the game.

### 2. Green Forest
A peaceful forest filled with hidden treasures, beginner enemies, and secret missions.

### 3. Crystal Cave
A mysterious underground cave containing rare resources and challenging puzzles.

### 4. Desert of Doom
A dangerous desert with powerful enemies, sandstorms, and hidden temples.

### 5. Frozen Kingdom
A snowy world where players unlock new abilities and face stronger bosses.

### 6. Volcano Arena
A high-risk battlefield with lava, elite enemies, and rare rewards.

### 7. Sky City
A floating city filled with advanced missions and flying enemies.

### 8. Cyber Laboratory
A futuristic research center where players encounter robots, AI enemies, and advanced coding challenges.

### 9. Shadow Realm
A dark dimension where only skilled players can survive.

### 10. Bug King's Fortress
The final battlefield where players fight the Bug King and save JavaVerse.
